FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our job portal services

JOBSEEKER

About JOB-Assam ?

JOB-Assam is a job portal site where you can find your suitable jobs easily and fastly.

How can i search for jobs ?

whenever jobs are available, JOB-Assam has posted that jobs on various social sites like facebook, linked IN, twitter etc. From there you can directly apply to jobs. No need to register, no need to pay any registration fee.

How to apply for jobs?

According to your requirement you can direct apply jobs from social sites. you can see similar jobs also, if you find relevant you can apply as you want.

How do i know i am being selected/shortlisted for a job ?

If you are selected, you will get a notification via email or given contact number.

How do i submit my resume ?

When completing the details of the online application form, you will be asked to attach your CV, so make sure you have it ready.

Can i apply more than one job ?

If you meet qualifications and experience necessary for that position then you may apply for more than one vacancy by completing the application requirements. Everyday new and active jobs are posted.

If i do not find a suitable vacancy, what can i do ?

If you do not find a suitable job, we encourage you to see job details every day.
